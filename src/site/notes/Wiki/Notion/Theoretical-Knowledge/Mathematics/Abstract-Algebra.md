# 1. Group

## 1. Basic Concept

### (1) Group

**(G, ·) group $\iff$**
* ① 结合律: $\forall a,b,c \in G, a·(b·c)=(a·b)·c$  
* ② 单位元: $\exists e \in G, \forall a \in G, a·e=e·a=a$
* ③ 逆元: $\forall a \in G, \exists a^{-1} \in G, a·a^{-1}=a^{-1}·a=e$

### (2) Abel Group

**(G, ·) group $\iff$**
* ① 结合律: $\forall a,b,c \in G, a·(b·c)=(a·b)·c$  
* ② 单位元: $\exists e \in G, \forall a \in G, a·e=e·a=a$
* ③ 逆元: $\forall a \in G, \exists a^{-1} \in G, a·a^{-1}=a^{-1}·a=e$
* ④ 交换律: $\forall a,b \in G, a·b=b·a$
